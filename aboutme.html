About me

Hello! My name is Duc Vuong or you can just call me DV.
I’m a freelance designer and a digital artist from Vietnam. I enjoy creating design for digital products on every platform. Additionally i also create website with HTML, CSS, JS or with other website development services.

I have graduated from Hanoi University of Industrial Fine Art with bachelor degree. Initially I have earned 5 years experience in branding design, print design and illustration during working in agencies and start-ups. In early 2018, I had my interest in designing Game Concept Art and Game User Interface when i joined a mobile game studio. During that time i had opportunity to work on Mobile Management App projects and i constantly focused on UI/UX Research and UI Design. I have learned myself Material Design and Human Interface Guidelines. After that i switch completely to working on Applications because to me each Application has it own important role and can affect significantly to many aspects of human ‘s life. When i worked with dev teams, I have learned HTML and CSS in order to understand how my design works when it ‘s in practice. Therefore I know how to make designing and coding works to be more productive together and less conflict.

Today, my main focus is designing accessible digital products & services for clients.

If you have a project you want to discuss, feel free to Get In Touch with me and we can find the best way to help you to take your project forward.
